Background and Context

Who is Lord Krishna?


Krishna is a major god in Hinduism, worshipped as the supreme god himself. Being the eight incarnation of Vishnu, Krishna is a god of love, compassion and protection.

Why is the Festival Celebrated?


Janmashtami is the festival celebrating birth of the Krishna god, which is on the 8th day of the dark fortnight of the month of Bhadrapada, which is usually either August or September.
